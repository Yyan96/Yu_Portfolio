Emo Touch
This is an experimental project exploring the possibilities of how computers can sense and understand human feelings and touch.I use a GSR sensor and FSR sensor to transmit signal changes to Touch Designer through Arduino to simulate human touch and emotional changes in computer eyes through vision, and I also use a servo motor to simulate the computer's touch feedback to humans.
Project Details / Background
In daily interpersonal communication, touch, as a sensory experience, is closely related to our psychology and culture. Touch is an important way for us to perceive the world. Many researchers refer to the pleasant tactile sensation caused by C tactile target stimulation as emotional touch (Gordon et al., 2013; McGlone et al., 2014; Perini et al., 2015).
When thinking about how to express tactile emotional changes through computers, I thought of generative art. The second is tactile feedback to simulate how computer touch human.
So, I used Arduino to use GSR to detect changes in skin surface conductivity and transmit the signal to Touchdesigner to affect the image's colour and speed changes to simulate the computer's emotional changes. I also used FSR to influence the movement direction of the servo motors to simulate the computer's touch mode.
