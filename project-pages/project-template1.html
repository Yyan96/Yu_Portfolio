Air Note
This is a gesture control music and painting interaction project based on handsfree.js. Users can use hand gestures to play note to create a song in front of the computer's camera.
Project Details / Background
This is a virtual guzheng made based on p5js. The colour matching is inspired by a Chinese animation work, "Deep Sea", and the background simulates the waves in the sea.
After turning on the Webcam, the user can interact with the camera through the fingers of both hands. Touching the fingertips of the thumb with the other four fingertips will trigger different notes and leave different colours on the canvas. You can also replace the voice with other instruments for simulated playing.
