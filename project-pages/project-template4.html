Fertilizer
We used Unity and Mocap to make this game. The game explores the relationship between the body and virtual space and the interaction and control between players. The whole game's story conveys our stance against war and the importance of protecting the planet.
Project Details / Background

Core Mechanism

Player1 will wear motion-capture suit. We will transmit the body data to the game scene in real-time. The body will form the terrain in the game. Player2 will play the role of slime mold to control the computer and use the body posture of player1 to pass through the obstacles in the game.

Story Background

After a nuclear war, humans almost became extinct due to their excessive dependence on and abuse of nuclear energy. Highly intelligent slime molds became the new masters of the planet and parasitized and enslaved all flora and fauna and humans. However, the earth has been in a nuclear winter for decades, with no visible sun, torn land, and polluted seas. The player controls the protagonist, a slime mold named No.996, who was sent by the "Central" to an abandoned sea transfer station to restart the equipment and provide energy for the group. It will manipulate a human "tool," numbered 007, to collect energy fragments and restart the transfer station.
