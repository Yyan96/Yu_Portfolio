My Background / About Me
From 2014 to 2018, I studied Landscape Architecture at Northeast Forestry University in Harbin, Heilongjiang Province, China.
After graduation, I worked as a second-grade Chinese teacher and class teacher in a primary school in a small village in Baise City, Guangxi Province. I was also in charge of English and art courses. I then ended two years of teaching life in July 2020. I am entering Goldsmiths, University of London, in 2022 and studying for an MA.
